Science Communication

I am a lecturer in Science Communication. I specialise in online engagement and digital projects, as well as the use of cultural transmission experiments to study the communication of sicence. I'm also interested in the challenges of communicating computer science, particularly methods to engage children and other populations in how to code, and public engagement with digital rights.

Language Evolution

My PhD and the majority of my previous research (and some current research) is on language evolution. How did human language get to be how it is? I am particularly interested in the emergence of linguistic structure (phonology, morphology and syntax) in both humans and non-human animals, and how modality (speech or sign), iconicity and social factors influence linguistic structure.
